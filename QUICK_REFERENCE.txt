💡 MYERSON CRITICAL VALUE FORMULA:

p_i(r) = r_i · a_i(r) - ∫₀^{r_i} a_i(z, r_{-i}) dz

Where:
  p_i(r) = payment for slice i
  r_i = reported demand
  a_i(r) = allocated PRBs
  z = integration variable (0 to r_i)

This formula ensures:
  - Truthful reporting yields max payoff
  - No incentive to misreport
  - Unique payment (up to constant)
